2) "Crucible Event Construct" & "Fate Crucible Event Construct" Research Instructions

- "Crucible" Construct Research Rules

• Guarantee Rules: The guarantee rules for "Crucible Event" Research are identical to those of the "Themed Construct" Research. You can perform research using [Event Construct R&D Tickets].

• Guarantee Gauge Inheritance Rules: The guarantee gauge is inherited between pools of the same type. For example, after a "Crucible Event Construct Research" has ended, its guarantee progress will be inherited by the next "Crucible Event Construct Research".

- "Crucible" Construct Research Reward Details

• Obtain phase rewards by performing "Crucible Event" Research.

• Phase Reward Rules: For every 20 researches performed, an additional phase reward can be obtained, up to 45 times. Rewards include but are not limited to: 6★ Weapon [Hear the Bell], the exclusive CUB [Grand Duke], Event Construct R&D Tickets, and more.

• During each period, the "Crucible Event Construct" and "Fate Crucible Event Construct" Research Pools share the same pull count progress.

• When each period of "Crucible Event" Research ends, the phase reward progress will be reset. When the next "Crucible Event" Research begins, the pull count will start accumulating anew. Resetting phase reward progress does not affect the guarantee counter.

• When each period of "Crucible Event" Research ends, unclaimed rewards will be sent via in-game mail. Please claim them promptly.
