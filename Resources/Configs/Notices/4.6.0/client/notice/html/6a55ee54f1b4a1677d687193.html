PRE-DOWNLOAD NOW AVAILABLE


Dear Commandant,


The pre-download for the new version of Punishing: Gray Raven is now available. You can access this feature via the Main Interface Entrance or through the terminal menu at the bottom right corner of the Main Interface.


How to Pre-Download

(Note: Pre-download requires a large amount of storage. We recommend using a Wi-Fi connection.)

1. Enter the pre-download page via the Main Interface Entrance or the secondary menu at the bottom right corner;

2. Tap the [Pre-download] button in the bottom right corner of the screen. You may choose between normal or full-speed download modes to begin downloading the new version’s resources;

3. During the download, you can switch freely between download modes;

4. Once the download is complete, you can check the status message in the terminal text ticker on the Main Interface.


Frequently Asked Questions


Q: Does pre-download support playing while downloading?

A: Yes. In normal mode, you can continue playing while downloading. In full-speed mode, the game interface will remain on the download screen, and no other operations can be performed.


Q: Will the download continue if the game is running in the background?

A: No. The download will pause automatically. You can resume it by re-entering the pre-download screen.


Q: If the download is interrupted (e.g., exiting the game or force closing it), will the downloaded data be lost?

A: No. As long as the pre-download feature is still available, you can resume the download. If you do not finish pre-downloading before the version update ends, the game will automatically check your local files and download the remaining resources upon game launch.


Q: If my Wi-Fi disconnects during the pre-download, will mobile data be used?

A: No. If the Wi-Fi connection drops, the system will automatically pause the download and display a notification in the terminal text ticker. Once the Wi-Fi is restored, you can resume the download from the pre-download screen.


Q: If I complete the pre-download, will I still need to download anything after maintenance ends?

A: Possibly. Even after completing the pre-download, some new resources may still need to be downloaded after maintenance, depending on the final content of the update.


If you encounter any issues during the pre-download process, feel free to contact us via the [Feedback] button in [Character Info] or through other official customer support channels. Thank you!
