Milestone Rewards:

Upon performing a certain number of researches, you will obtain the following rewards.

- 50 times: Weapon Coating [Crystalline Whimsy]

- 70 times: Portrait [Fluttering Ballet]

- 90 times: Portrait Frame [Fluttering Ballet]


== Event Rules ==

1. Research Token

Each research consumes [Plume Wish Invitation] *1, which can be exchanged for Black Card *175 or Rainbow Card *5.

2. Reward Types

Rewards are categorized into 2 types: Special Reward and Common Reward. Each research will determine the reward type according to the research rates.

3. Research Rates:

Special Reward: 1%

Common Reward: 99%

Every 20 researches guarantees 1 Special Reward. Special Rewards are limited in number; you can not obtain the same Special Reward repeatedly.

4. Token Expiry

After the event, unused [Plume Wish Invitations] will be converted into Basic Construct R&D Tickets at a ratio of 1:175.


== Bonus Features ==

Upon obtaining the coating [Fluttering Ballet], you will unlock the following content:

- Sightline tracing feature on Main interface and Interact interface.

- Exclusive animations and voice lines for Battle Start and Battle Over.

- New Ultima Ring appearance and exclusive [Idle] animation.

- Equip [Fluttering Ballet] coating to unlock [Display Pose 5]. Equip [Fluttering Ballet] and apply Main Interface Scene [Path of Unclouded Hearts] at the same time to unlock [Display Pose 4].



For more details, please refer to the in-game event page.
