== Phylotree Nexus Research Note ==

- This session of the [Phylotree Nexus] banner includes [Inverse Crown Event Construct] and [Fate Inverse Crown Event Construct] researches.

- The [Phylotree Nexus] research shares the same rules with [Themed Construct] research. You can use Event Construct R&D Tickets to perform research in this banner.

- The [Phylotree Nexus] and the [Themed Construct] researches share the same Guarantee Gauge, and the gauge counting is shared mutually as well. For example, the [Inverse Crown Event Construct] and [Themed Construct] researches share the same Guarantee Gauge, while the [Fate Inverse Crown Event Construct] and the [Fate Themed Construct] researches share the same Guarantee Gauge.

- New Characters from the [Phylotree Nexus] research can not be obtained from other research banners. These limited new characters will rerun in the [Phylotree Nexus] research in future versions.

- The inver-shards of characters obtained from the [Phylotree Nexus] research share the same acquisition method as other S-Rank Omniframes obtained from the [Themed Construct] research, which includes obtaining the same character repeatedly, redeeming in the [Phantom Pain Shop] with [Phantom Pain Scars], and redeeming the inver-shards directly in the [Shard Shop].

- The accompanying 6★ weapons and CUBs of the [Phylotree Nexus] characters share the same rerun schedule with the characters, and can be obtained in the [Phylotree Nexus Weapon Target] and the [Phylotree Nexus CUB] researches, respectively.
