== Phylotree Nexus Research Rerun Note ==

- This session of the [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] banner includes [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] and [Fate Phylotree Nexus Rerun] researches.

- The [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] research shares the same rules with [Themed Construct] research. You can use Event Construct R&D Tickets to perform research in this banner.

- The [Phylotree Nexus Rerun], [Phylotree Nexus], and [Themed Construct] researches share the same Guarantee Gauge, and the pity count carries over between pools of the same type. For example, the [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] and [Inverse Crown Event Construct] researches share the same Guarantee Gauge, while the [Fate Phylotree Nexus Rerun] and the [Fate Inverse Crown Event Construct] researches share the same Guarantee Gauge.

- Characters from the [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] research can not be obtained from other research banners. These limited characters will rerun in the [Phylotree Nexus] series researches in future versions.

- The inver-shards of characters obtained from the [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] research share the same acquisition method as other S-Rank Omniframes obtained from the [Themed Construct] research, which includes obtaining the same character repeatedly, redeeming in the [Phantom Pain Shop] with [Phantom Pain Scars], and redeeming the inver-shards directly in the [Shard Shop].

- The accompanying 6★ weapons and CUBs of the [Phylotree Nexus Rerun] characters share the same rerun schedule with the characters, and can be obtained in the [Phylotree Nexus Weapon Target] and the [Phylotree Nexus CUB] researches, respectively.
